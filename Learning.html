My Learning

I learned how to use variables to represent unknown values, form and simplify algebraic expressions, solve linear equations and inequalities, and understand functions. Graphing linear equations helped me understand slope and y-intercept. I practiced factoring techniques, solving quadratic equations using various methods, and applying algebra to real-world problems involving rates, money, mixtures, and geometry.
